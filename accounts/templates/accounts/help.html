Account & Access

How to log in

Visit the login page and enter your username and password provided by your administrator during onboarding. If your credentials are not working, contact your admin to verify your account is active.

Your account must be set to active by an admin before you can log in.

How to register a new account

Self-registration is not supported. All accounts are created by an administrator during onboarding. If you are a new employee, contact your admin and they will set up your account and provide your login credentials.

How to reset your password

If you have forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password link on the login page to contact your admin. There is no automated reset link — your admin will reset your password to a temporary default and notify you.

Automated password reset emails are not currently supported. All resets go through your administrator.

Account lockout & recovery

If you are unable to access your account, it may have been deactivated. Use the Contact Support page to notify your admin, who can reactivate your account from the admin dashboard.

User roles & access permissions

There are four roles in the portal:

  • Admin — full access including user management, CRUD for all entities, and admin dashboard
  • Department Head — access to department-level views and reports
  • Team Leader — access to team management and project views
  • User — standard read access to the portal
Your role is assigned by an admin and determines which pages and features you can access.

Changing your password

Once logged in, you can update your password from your profile page. If you are locked out before you can do this, contact your administrator.

Getting Started

Overview of the system

The SKY Engineering Portal is an internal tool for managing teams, departments, projects, and dependencies across the engineering organisation. It provides reporting, messaging, and scheduling in one place.

Understanding the UI layout

The portal has three main navigation areas:

  • Top navbar — SKY logo, portal name, and logout button
  • Left sidebar — main navigation menu linking to all sections
  • Main content area — the primary view for each page
On smaller screens, the left sidebar collapses into a hamburger menu.

Navigating the main menu

The sidebar menu contains links to: Dashboard, Teams, Departments, Organisation, Messages, Schedule, Reports, Profile, and Admin (admin users only).

Accessibility features

The portal is designed to be responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile screen sizes. Navigation menus collapse to hamburger menus on smaller screens for ease of use.

👥

Teams & Departments

Viewing teams

Navigate to the Teams page from the sidebar. You can see all teams in the organisation, their department, team leader, and members.

Team leaders

Each team should have a designated team leader. Teams without a leader are flagged in the Reports dashboard under the leadership coverage indicator.

Teams with no assigned leader will appear as a gap in the leadership coverage progress indicator on the reports page.

Departments

Departments group related teams together. You can view all departments and the teams within them from the Departments page.

Dependencies

Teams can have upstream or downstream dependencies on other teams. These are visualised in the Reports section as a pie chart showing the ratio of upstream to downstream dependencies across the organisation.

📂

Projects

Viewing projects

Projects are listed under each team. You can view a project's name, status, associated team, codebase URL, and Jira board link.

Project status

Each project has one of the following statuses:

  • Active — currently in progress
  • On Hold — temporarily paused
  • Completed — finished

Codebase & Jira links

Each project may have an associated codebase URL and Jira board link. These are displayed as clickable links in the project table and open in a new tab.

💌

Messaging

Sending a message

Go to Messages in the sidebar and click + New Message. Select a recipient from the dropdown, enter a subject and your message, then click Send. You can also save it as a draft to finish later.

Inbox, sent & drafts

Your inbox shows all messages sent to you. The Sent tab shows messages you have sent. The Drafts tab shows messages you have saved but not yet sent. Unread messages appear in bold in your inbox.

Contacting admin (pre-login)

If you cannot log in, use the Contact Support page accessible from the login screen to send a message directly to all administrators. You do not need to be logged in to use this form.

Your message is delivered to all admin users via the internal messaging system.
📊

Reports

Dashboard overview

The Reports page gives a high-level view of the organisation at a glance, including total teams, departments, projects, and leadership coverage.

Charts & graphs

The reports page includes the following visualisations:

  • Leadership coverage — progress circle showing percentage of teams with an assigned leader
  • Teams per department — bar chart comparing team counts across departments
  • Projects per team — ring chart showing project distribution by team
  • Dependency direction — pie chart showing upstream vs downstream dependency ratio
  • Automated insights — summary of key findings from the data

Exporting data

You can download the underlying report data as a CSV file directly from the reports page using the export button.

📅

Scheduling

Scheduling a meeting

Go to the Schedule page from the sidebar. Fill in the meeting details including title, date, time, and participants, then submit to create the meeting entry.

Viewing your schedule

The Schedule page lists all upcoming and past meetings associated with your account. You can view meeting details by selecting an entry from the list.

⚙

Admin

Admin dashboard

The Admin dashboard is accessible only to users with the Admin role. It provides CRUD management for users, teams, departments, projects, and dependencies.

Managing users

Admins can create, edit, deactivate, and delete user accounts. When a new user is created, a one-time auto-generated password is displayed — copy and share this with the user during onboarding as it cannot be retrieved again.

The generated password is only shown once. If lost, use the reset password function instead.

Resetting a user's password

From the admin dashboard, locate the user and use the reset password option. This resets their password to a temporary default. Inform the user and ask them to change it after logging in.

Managing teams, departments & dependencies

Admins can add, edit, and delete teams, departments, and dependencies from the admin panel. Changes are reflected immediately across the portal.

Troubleshooting

I cannot log in

Check that your username and password are correct. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you have forgotten your password or your account has been deactivated, use the Contact Support page to notify your admin.

A page is not loading correctly

Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If it still does not work, report the issue via the Contact Support page.

I am not seeing data I expect

Your access level may restrict what data is visible. If you believe you are missing access that you should have, contact your admin to review your role and permissions.

Reporting a bug

If you encounter unexpected behaviour or errors, use the Contact Support page and select System Issue as the reason. Include the page affected and your browser details to help the admin investigate.

The more detail you include, the faster the issue can be resolved.

Contact support

For anything not covered here, use the Contact Support page accessible from the login screen or the Help page. Your message will be delivered to all administrators.
